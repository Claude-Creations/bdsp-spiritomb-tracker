Spiritomb NPC Tracker

Track which NPCs you've talked to in the Grand Underground to unlock Spiritomb in Pokémon Brilliant Diamond & Shining Pearl.

Progress

0 / 32

You need 32 of 35 NPCs.

  1. Get the Explorer Kit from the Underground Man in Eterna City.
  2. Obtain an Odd Keystone — find one on Route 208 (from the Black Belt NPC), dig one up in the Grand Underground, or find the hidden one in Twinleaf Town.
  3. Go to Route 209 and interact with the Hallowed Tower (the broken stone tower) to place the Odd Keystone.
  4. Enter the Grand Underground and talk to 32 unique named NPCs. Use the checklist below to track your progress. Sphere Traders do NOT count.
  5. Once you've talked to 32 NPCs, return to the Hallowed Tower on Route 209 and interact with it to encounter Spiritomb (Lv. 25).
To respawn NPCs quickly, enter and exit a Pokémon Hideaway. Each area has NPCs that spawn more frequently there, but any NPC can appear anywhere.
